One Accident.
One Intelligent Flow.

From roadside crash to verified payout, every step is automated, scored, and transparently tracked.

STEP 01

Incident Details

Claim filed directly at the accident spot with vehicle details, crash context, driver identity, and precise location.

STEP 02

Document Upload

RC, DL, policy documents, bills, and expected claim amount verified instantly using OCR.

STEP 03

AI Damage & Cost Analysis

AI analyzes on-spot photos and videos, estimates repair cost, checks policy limits, and generates fraud score.

STEP 04

Fraud Intelligence Engine

Claims auto-routed based on AI risk score.

GREEN (Score < 30)

  • ✔ Documents verified
  • ✔ Driver matches policy
  • ✔ Within limits

YELLOW (30–70)

  • ⚠ Unknown garage bill
  • ⚠ Late reporting
  • ⚠ Driver mismatch

RED (> 70)

  • ✖ High fraud risk
  • ✖ Policy mismatch
  • ✖ Inflated cost
STEP 05

Claim Tracking & History

Real-time status tracking, fraud score visibility, payouts, and full claim history.

STEP 06

Insurer Dashboard

Insurers monitor daily, weekly, monthly claims, approvals, pending reviews, and fraud rejections.
